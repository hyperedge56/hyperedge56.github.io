Childhood Dream

 When I was young, I was always very sick. I had a weak immune system and I was always in the hospital. Because of that, I became used to seeing my doctor every week. Over time, they became symbols of safety and discipline in my eyes. I wanted to become a doctor because of that experience. I wanted to help people and give them the safety and support they needed. I think the first kind of doctor I wanted to be was a surgeon. It was only recently that I started looking into psychiatry and psychology.
